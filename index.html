What is a YouTube Thumbnail?

A YouTube Thumbnail is a small preview image that appears before opening a video. It serves as a cover image for the video, automatically generated by YouTube or manually uploaded by the video uploader.

Types of YouTube Thumbnails:

  • 1. Auto-Generated Thumbnails – YouTube selects a frame from the video and creates a thumbnail.
  • 2. Custom Thumbnails – Creators can upload a custom-designed thumbnail.
  • 3. High-Quality Thumbnails – YouTube generates HD (1280×720) or Max Resolution (1920×1080) thumbnails for some videos.
